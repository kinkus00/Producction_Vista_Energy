Assumptions & Warnings

All material departures, WI overrides, and data flags.

FormulaBOE conversion applied as specified
Oil boe/d = Σ(prod_pet) × 6.2898 × WI ÷ days. Gas boe/d = Σ(prod_gas) × 6.2898 × WI ÷ days. Argentine SE prod_pet in m³, prod_gas in Mm³. Actual calendar days per month. No interpolation or synthetic data.
ColumnsColumn mapping — CSV vs. spec
"Column J/K" → prod_pet / prod_gas. "Column C (Field)" → areapermisoconcesion for all non-operated (Aconcagua, PAE). Each concession may encompass multiple areayacimiento — e.g. ENTRE LOMAS concession includes Charco Bayo, El Caracol, Borde Mocho, Piedras Blancas, Lomas de Ocampo, Los Alamos, La Pista + Entre Lomas proper.
Field filterJAGÜEL DE LOS MACHOS RN — Aconcagua concession
JAGÜEL DE LOS MACHOS RN is an areapermisoconcesion operated by Aconcagua, containing 7 yacimientos: Tapera Avendaño, Las Lagunas (+Sur, Norte, Este), Puesto Morales, and Médano. All included in non-op bucket.
WI overrideYPF / La Amarga Chica — pre-Apr-2025 excluded
WI = 0% before Apr-2025. All LAC production before Apr-2025 omitted. From Apr-2025: 50% WI on oil and gas.
WI overrideAconcagua — time-varying WI schedule
Base WI = 20% (oil & gas) from Sep-2025. Q4-2024 to Aug-2025: oil = 40%, gas = 0% (oil-only). Prior to Q4-2024: oil = 40%, gas = 40%. Filtered on areapermisoconcesion. Verified vs. user June 2025 numbers: 2,308.5 boe/d (user: 2,308.3 ✓).
WI overrideWell-level overrides — 40 BPO wells (Trafigura JV), pre-2025
Pre-2025: 28 wells at 80% (162590–593, 162742–745, 163138–141, 163249–251, 163392–395, 163491–494, 163810, 163873–876) and 12 wells at 75% (164300–303, 164445–448, 164622–625). From 2025: asset-level 100% resumes.
CoverageMarch 2026 partial — truncated
Vista/YPF/PAE had not filed March 2026. Dashboard ends at Feb-2026.
Methodology"New wells" = first-ever-active in dataset
Jan-2024 starting population (376 wells) excluded. For YPF-LAC, wells active before Apr-2025 are NOT counted as "new" when entering Vista's scope. Avg IP = average net boe/d of new wells in their debut month.
Compliance checklist
  • Oil & gas separate: prod_pet × 6.2898 × WI ÷ days and prod_gas × 6.2898 × WI ÷ days.
  • Actual calendar days per month.
  • WI applied at row level before aggregation.
  • Operated: Coirón Amargo Norte = 96.77%; Águila Mora = 98%; all other = 100% (from 2025).
  • Pre-2025 BPO well overrides: 28@80%, 12@75%.
  • Aconcagua: 40/40 (pre-Q4-2024) → 40/0 (Q4-2024 to Aug-2025) → 20/20 (Sep-2025+). Concession filter.
  • PAE ACAMBUCO: 1.5%.
  • YPF LAC: 50% from Apr-2025; excluded before.
  • No fabricated data. March 2026 excluded (partial).
